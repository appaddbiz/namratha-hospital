Welcome to
Namratha Hospital

Namratha Hospital in Bangalore, India, established in 1989, is renowned for its personalized patient care across a range of medical specialties.

It offers comprehensive services including general medicine, pediatrics, gynecology, obstetrics, surgery, and a 24x7 emergency department. Accredited by NABH, it ensures high standards of quality care, with a dedicated team focused on patients' well-being regardless of financial status.
