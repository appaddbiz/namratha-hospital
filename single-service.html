OBSTETRICS AND GYNAECOLOGY

Namratha Hospital is renowned for its comprehensive and patient-centered approach to women's health, offering a wide range of services in the fields of Obstetrics and Gynaecology. The hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by experienced specialists dedicated to providing high-quality care.

The Obstetrics and Gynaecology Department at Namratha Hospital provides comprehensive and compassionate care for women at all stages of life.

Our expert team offers a wide range of services, from prenatal care and safe, supportive labor and delivery, to postpartum care ensuring the health of both mother and baby. In gynecology, we address routine care, manage menstrual disorders, and treat conditions like uterine fibroids and endometriosis. We also offer advanced fertility services, minimally invasive surgeries, and cancer care.

If we cannot assist, we can provide referrals or advice about the type of practitioner you require. We are committed to being the region’s premier healthcare network by providing patient-centered care that inspires clinical and service excellence, making us the first and best choice for our patients, employees, physicians, employers, volunteers and communities.
