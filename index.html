Namratha Hospital

"A Legacy of Compassionate Healthcare Excellence"

In the heart of Mahalaxmi Layout, Bangalore, Namratha Hospital stand as a stalwart in the healthcare landscape. Established in 1989 by the visionary duo, Dr. Sreenath K and Dr. Sandhya Sreenath, this institution has grown into a trusted name, providing a diverse range of medical services with a primary focus on obstetrics and gynecology. With a steadfast commitment to delivering appropriate and ethical treatment at affordable prices, Namratha Hospitals have become synonymous with compassionate care, earning the motto, "Home Away from Home."

For over three decades, Namratha Hospitals have been at the forefront of healthcare, setting benchmarks for quality, compassion, and innovation. The founders, Dr. Sreenath K and Dr. Sandhya Sreenath, envisioned a healthcare institution that not only addressed the medical needs of the community but also embraced patients with warmth and understanding.

Health Tips

Simple Steps to a Healthier You

Unmasking IBS, Empowering Digestive Health.

This April, Namratha Hospital stands in solidarity with those affected by Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS). We are committed to raising awareness and providing exceptional care for individuals managing this condition. Our expert team offers specialized treatments and compassionate support to enhance the quality of life for those living with IBS.

In honor of IBS Awareness Month, we offer exclusive health packages, free consultations, and educational programs focused on early diagnosis, effective symptom management, and holistic care. Join us in advocating for better digestive health and well-being for everyone affected by IBS.
