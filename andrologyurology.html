Andrology & Urology

Andrology and urology at Namratha Hospital encompass specialized medical services focused on male reproductive health and urological disorders. Our dedicated team of andrologists and urologists provides comprehensive care, ranging from routine screenings to advanced treatments for complex conditions.

At Namratha Hospital, we prioritize patient-centered care, providing compassionate support and personalized treatment plans. Our goal is to enhance the quality of life for our patients by addressing their urological and andrological concerns effectively and with utmost professionalism.

Key Features of Andrology & Urology

  • Comprehensive diagnostic services for male reproductive health
  • Specialized treatments for male infertility, erectile dysfunction, and hormone imbalances
  • Advanced reproductive technology and state-of-the-art diagnostic tools
  • Individualized treatment plans tailored to specific patient needs
  • Expertise in managing complex andrological disorders
  • Comprehensive care for urinary tract disorders and male reproductive system conditions
  • Treatment of kidney stones, urinary tract infections, prostate disorders, bladder issues, and urologic cancers
  • Minimally invasive and surgical treatment options
  • Multidisciplinary approach integrating medical and surgical expertise
  • Patient-centered care with emphasis on patient education and support
