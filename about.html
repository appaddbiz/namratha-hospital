About Us

Namratha Hospital

"A Legacy of Compassionate Healthcare Excellence"

In the heart of Mahalaxmi Layout, Bangalore, Namratha Hospital stand as a stalwart in the healthcare landscape. Established in 1989 by the visionary duo, Dr. Sreenath K and Dr. Sandhya Sreenath, this institution has grown into a trusted name, providing a diverse range of medical services with a primary focus on obstetrics and gynecology. With a steadfast commitment to delivering appropriate and ethical treatment at affordable prices, Namratha Hospitals have become synonymous with compassionate care, earning the motto, "Home Away from Home."

It offers comprehensive services including general medicine, pediatrics, gynecology, obstetrics, surgery, and a 24x7 emergency department. Accredited by NABH, it ensures high standards of quality care, with a dedicated team focused on patients' well-being regardless of financial status.

Vision

Dr. Sreenath K and Dr. Sandhya Sreenath, the visionary founders of Namratha Hospitals, envisioned a healthcare institution that would not only provide top-notch medical services but also nurture a culture of empathy, compassion, and ethical values. Their leadership has been instrumental in shaping the institution's ethos and instilling a sense of purpose that resonates throughout the organization.

Mission

Namratha Hospitals proudly uphold a mission rooted in the principles of appropriate and ethical treatment at affordable prices. The institution recognizes the responsibility to provide high-quality healthcare without compromising on integrity, ensuring that every patient receives the care they deserve, irrespective of their economic background.



Core Tenets Of Our Mission

Appropriate Treatment

Namratha Hospitals uphold the highest medical ethics standards, offering personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's needs. This ensures interventions are effective and aligned with the individual's best health interests, promoting optimal care and patient well-being.

Ethical Practices

Integrity is the cornerstone of our mission. All medical practices, from diagnosis to treatment, adhere to the strictest ethical standards. Our commitment to transparency fosters trust between healthcare providers and patients, creating a foundation for a lasting doctor-patient relationship.

Affordability

Healthcare should be accessible to all, and Namratha Hospitals take pride in offering services at affordable prices. By implementing cost-effective measures without compromising on quality, the institution ensures that financial constraints do not impede access to essential medical care.
